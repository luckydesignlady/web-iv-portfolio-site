Ben´s Butterfly Garden Identity

Hawgs for Dogs is a nonprofit org. dedicated to raising money for Guide Dogs of America, who provide seeing eye dogs to visually impaired and blind people at no cost. The Lincoln, NE chapter of Hawgs for Dogs needed a new image, to help potential riders identify with Hawgs for Dogs over other rides happening the same weekend. It is important for the mark to speak to riding enthusiasts, specifically those in the 35–65 age group who enjoy riding for pleasure, as well as raising money for various causes.

Now that the Lincoln, NE chapter of Hawgs for Dogs has a strong visual identity, they need a set of materials to use for all of the materials they put out throughought the year. Letters must be sent to past and prospective donors for raffle items and monetary donations, as well as thank-you notes to thank businesses who sponsor the poker run, so a letterhead was created, along with envelopes. The organizers also needed new business cards that matched their new visual identity.
