NavilluS family photography Logo, Style guide

Tonja Sullivan is the heart and soul of NavilluS Family Photography. Tonja has made it her passion to capture special moments in time, so families can have memories to last a lifetime.

The logo is made up of a sketched camera overlaid with the name NavilluS family photography. The ‘N’ and the ‘S’ are both capitalized, because Navillus is Sullivan backwards, so it is important to use both capitals any time that the name is used.
