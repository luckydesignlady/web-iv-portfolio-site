Karaoke Kraze Mobile Songbook App

Karaoke Kraze is a small Karaoke Company based in Lincoln, Nebraska. The company currently uses song books, made of plastic binders and hundreds of sheets of paper, which are inefficient because there arenâ€™t usually enough books for each table in the bar, and it can be very costly to reprint the entire songbook each time the company updates its song inventory.

Karaoke Kraze needs a mobile app to serve as a songbook for karaoke singers, to help them quickly and easily decide on a song to sing. The target audience for this app is men and woman, ages 21-35, who go to local bars to sing karaoke. They like to go out on the weekends, drink, and have a good time with friends. Because users are likely to be slightly intoxicated when using this app, it needed to be very intuitive, and easy to use, without too many frills to add confusion.
