Hawgs for Dogs

Logo, Promotional Materials, Corporate ID System

Hawgs for Dogs is a nonprofit organization dedicated to raising money for Guide Dogs of America, who, in turn, provide seeing eye dogs to visually impaired and blind individuals at absolutely no cost to them. The Lincoln, NE chapter of Hawgs for Dogs has held their event for the last six years, and it has always been successful, but donations werenâ€™t increasing at the rates they had hoped for. Hawgs for Dogs needed a new image, to help potential riders identify with Hawgs for Dogs over other rides happening the same weekend. In the summertime, there can be anywhere from one to five different poker runs benefitting different causes happening on the same weekend, so it is important for the mark to speak to riding enthusiasts, specifically those in the 35–65 age group who enjoy riding for pleasure, as well as raising money for various causes.

Now that the Lincoln, NE chapter of Hawgs for Dogs has a strong visual identity, they need a set of materials to use for all of the materials they put out throughought the year. Letters must be sent to past and prospective donors for raffle items and monetary donations, as well as thank-you notes to thank businesses who sponsor the poker run, so a letterhead was created, along with envelopes. The organizers also needed new business cards that matched their new visual identity.
