Hawgs for Dogs

Designer Rebrands Local Fundraiser

Hawgs for Dogs is an annual Poker Run that benefits Guide Dogs of America. While the event has grown in popularity every year, organizers wanted to provide a cohesive brand identity for riders. A number of materials have been created for Hawgs for Dogs, from a logo, to promotional posters for the ride, to T-shirts, to business cards.
