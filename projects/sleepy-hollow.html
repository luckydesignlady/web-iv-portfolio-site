Sleepy HollowRedesign of classic book makes reading more interesting for pre–teens.

Sleepy Hollow Book Cover/ Innards

Sleepy Hollow is a classic story by Washington Irving that needed to be updated to make it more appealing to readers in the pre-teen age group. These readers are more engaged by photographic imagery than simple typography or illustrations, so I used photoshop to recreate the scene at the end of the story, when they find Ichabod Crane’s horse wandering without him. I chose this scene because it creates a correlation between the cover, which readers see before they start, and the end of the story, to help keep young readers thinking about what they’ve just read.
